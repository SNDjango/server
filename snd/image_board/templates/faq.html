FAQ

Settings
1. How can I get a profile?
You must have a valid email address and then you can create a profile here.

2. How can I post a new picture?
You should be registered and then you can create a post here.

3. How can I change my password or my emailadress?
You can change it at your profile here.



Getting started
1. What does the tab "hot" mean?
It gathers the posts that have received most likes in your network.

2. What are Likes and how can I do this?
Likes show how many people like the post. Like a post by clicking on the thumbs up symbol.



About us
1. What is TU-Gag?
TU-Gag is an open source platform which can be implemented by any organization who wishes to create their own verison of "9GAG" for their organization.

2. Who are behind TU-Gag?
The members behind TU-Gaga belong to a small project team of 10 students of the TU Berlin. The project started in Spring 2017 as a part of a course.
