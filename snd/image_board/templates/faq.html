FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

Settings
1. How can I get a profile?
You must have a valid email address and then you can sign up for an account here.

2. How can I post a new picture?
You should be registered and then you can create a post here.

3. How can I change my password or my emailadress?
When logged in, you can change it on your profile page.



Getting Started
1. What does the tab "hot" mean?
"Hot" gathers the posts that have received most the likes within in your network.

2. What are Likes and how can I like sometihng?
Likes show how many people have pressed "like" on a certain post. You can like a post by clicking on the thumbs up symbol under the post.



About Us
1. What is TU-Gag?
TU-Gag is an open source platform which can be implemented by any organization who wishes to create their own verison of "9GAG" for their organization.

2. Who are behind TU-Gag?
The creators of TU-Gag belong to a small project team of 10 students of the TU Berlin. The project started in Spring 2017 as a part of the module ProgrammierPraktikum - Soziale Netzwerke.
