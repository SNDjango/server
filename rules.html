Rules

Tu-Gag users have to adhere to the following rules. If they do not, their profile will be deleted and Tu-Gag can allow this user to never register again.

The most important rules of TU-Gag.

  1. No pornography: Pornographic or sexual contributions are not tolerated.
  2. No violence, gory and harmful content: Content or comments that lead to certain violent acts are immediately deleted.
  3. No illegal activities: Contributions or comments which lead to illegal activities are not permitted. TU-Gag can report this to law enforcement.
  4. No personal and confidential information: Do not post any personal information about other users to protect their privacy.
  5. No hate speech and bullying: It is not permissible to post contributions that can exclude or injure people (eg due to their origin, race, religion, age, nationality, etc.).
  6. No copyright and trademark infringement: If you post pictures you have not created yourself, you must inform yourself about copyrights and urgently contribute the source. But Tu-Gag should be unique, so users are asked to post their own original images.

Tu-Gag is meant to be a fun entertainment. If everyone sticks to our little rules, we can have a great side against boredom here.
Thank you
